The Story That Started It All

In 1981, Yossi Ghinsberg was 22 years old — swept over a waterfall deep in the uncharted Bolivian Amazon and left completely alone. For three weeks he survived on instinct, will, and the refusal to give up. That story became the bestseller Jungle, a Hollywood film, and 25 years on the world's main stages. Google. Microsoft. MDRT. YPO. The All Blacks. Audiences of 8,000 have sat in absolute silence. He has been called the most unforgettable speaker they have ever seen. He simply tries to be honest.
