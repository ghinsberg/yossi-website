The Story That Started It All

In 1981, Yossi Ghinsberg was swept over a waterfall deep in the uncharted Bolivian Amazon and left completely alone. For 21 days he survived on instinct, will, and the refusal to stop. That story became Jungle — a bestselling book in 20 languages, a Hollywood film with Daniel Radcliffe — and 25 years on the world's main stages. Google. Microsoft. YPO. MDRT. The All Blacks. He has been called the most unforgettable speaker they have ever seen.
